To find out exactly what qualifies please check out the HMRC’s guidelines listed at : Claiming Research and Development tax reliefs Don’t worry! Your company will not be disqualified if your R&D has been focused on adapting or reinventing an existing product instead of creating something new. Risk is an essential element of R&D activity – risk of failing to achieve the desired outcome, or success in achieving a result but in a way which is not commercially viable. Whether or not the project succeeds does not determine whether it qualifies for the reliefs or not. R&D Tax relief may be given as a reduction of Corporation Tax due on profits or as a tax credit payable to the company by HMRC if the company has made a loss, or a mixture of tax relief and credit.
